What We Do

Iteka, Internet Technology Applications, is a company that is very enthusiastic about introducing the freshest, innovative designs that provide a rich user experience for the end user. Our responsive designs support many of the existing and upcoming technologies to include most mobile devices.

User experience is becoming more important than ever for companies to keep users on their site. Consumer sales of PC's are at an all-time low, do to more and more people utilizing their smart-phones and tablets to go online. With more than 55% of all web traffic now coming from mobile phones and tablets, your website needs to be responsive, look good and functional no matter what kind of device it’s being viewed on. Not only is responsive design important for the user experience, but it is becoming a requirement of web browsers for websites to be responsive.

We have designed websites from individual entrepreneurs to small mom and pop businesses and E-commerce solutions with storefront applications. Small startup businesses are more than welcome.

If you are looking for a cost effective, affordable, hand crafted custom designed website, then you need not look any further than Iteka, we do know what is required to bring your web presence to success on the ever changing Internet of today and the future...

The advancement in application and internet technology is growing at a very rapid pace. Iteka continues to move forward with these technologies, utilizing them to the fullest of our ability. Those who don't, will fall far behind in a very short period of time.

With the advanced continual updates of the modern day browsers, these technologies are being realized and taken to new limits allowing for a more interactive, positive internet experience. At Iteka, we move forward with these new challenges and continue to grow with them on a daily basis.

Our History

Iteka, Internet Technology Applications, was established in 1997, very near the start of the internet revolution which has changed radically since that time. We at Iteka are still devoted to producing an affordable, quality internet experience for our clients today and in the future.

From the beginning, Iteka has invested a large fortune in software and the continual updates to the software. To stay on the leading edge of the competitive market, Iteka has transitioned a major portion of our software to open source. This transition translates to a cost savings for our clients. The open source community is knocking very hard on the doors of the software giants.

Has our quality and productivity taken a hit from this transition? Not in the very least. Iteka is here for the long run. Transitions are what technology is about.
